Our Journey

From humble beginnings in 2005, SLR Installations has grown into a leading provider of innovative installation solutions. Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction has driven our success, allowing us to expand our services and expertise over the years.

Timeline

2005: Founding of SLR Installations

SLR Installations is established with a focus on residential installations.

2010: Expansion of Services

The company expands its services to include commercial and industrial installations.

2015: Reaching 50+ Projects

SLR Installations successfully completes over 50 projects, marking a significant milestone.

2020: New Headquarters

The company moves to a new, larger headquarters to accommodate its growing team.

Today: Industry Leader

SLR Installations is recognized as a leader in the installation industry, known for its quality and innovation.
